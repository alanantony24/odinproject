Lemonade Recipe

This lemonade is good on hot days, and very successful at lemonade stands.

Have this everyday to increase your electrolytes count. Perfect for hot and humid whether to make yourself feel refreshed

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 whole lemon
  • 1 table spoon Sugar
  • I teaspoon Salt

Steps to make

  1. Add in the water to a mug
  2. With a squeezer and squeeze the lemon into the mug. Avoid the seeds.
  3. Add salt and sugar into the water.
  4. Mix well and enjoy :)
